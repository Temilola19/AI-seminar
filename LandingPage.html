  • While influenza remains a fairly frequent disease, other illnesses with identical symptoms are few and far between, with variable degrees of lethality. Accurately identifying such disorders and prescribing appropriate treatment may be difficult in many regions of the world, since clinicians may not rely on testing every patient to ascertain the particular condition (rather, on tacit and explicit information). The suggested system is designed to collect and analyse patient symptoms in order to "suggest" a diagnosis. The system may also suggest non-medication remedies.
